Resources & Scam Safety Tips

Phishing Emails

Never click suspicious links in emails. Always check the sender address carefully before entering passwords or downloading files.

Investment Scams

If someone promises guaranteed profits or pressures you to invest quickly, it is likely a scam. Always research before sending money.

OTP and Banking Scams

Never share OTP, PIN, or banking details with anyone. Banks never ask for this information through calls or messages.

Job Scams

Be careful of job offers that ask for registration fees or training fees before giving work. Legitimate companies do not charge applicants.
