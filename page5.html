About Digital Protector

Our Mission

Digital Protector is a global scam awareness and reporting platform designed to help people stay safe from online fraud.

Online scams such as phishing attacks, fake investments, job scams, and financial fraud affect millions of people every year. Our goal is to create a public knowledge base where people can report scams and help others recognize threats before they become victims.

How This Platform Helps

Users can submit scam reports anonymously. These reports help build a community-driven database that allows others to check and learn about common scam techniques used around the world.

Privacy

We respect user privacy. Any optional contact information submitted by users remains private and is not displayed publicly on the website.

Disclaimer

Digital Protector is an independent awareness platform and is not affiliated with any government agency or law enforcement organization. For official complaints, users should contact the relevant government cybercrime authorities listed in the resources section.
